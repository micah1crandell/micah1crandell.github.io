Our Core Values

  • Excellence is our Offering
    • It isn’t perfectionism or a fear of mistakes. Excellence is bringing our best to honor God and to serve His people.
    • We believe that excellence honors God and inspires people.
  • We Bridge the Gap
    • We are the bridge between the stage and the eyes and ears of the people in the room and online.
    • Worship is more than song and singing. Worship is pointing and turning attention to God.
    • It’s easy to get caught up in the tasks of production, and even though those tasks are important, the only real goal we have is to help lead worship.
  • We Serve as a Team
    • We have a “We over Me” mentality.
    • We are one body with many roles, and each part matters. We communicate and support each other for the church. What we do is bigger than ourselves—we are here for the church.
    • Part of serving as one team means we pursue the same style and vision together.
