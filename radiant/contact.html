Frequently Asked Questions

Can I volunteer only once a month?

Yes, we are happy to accommodate your availability. If a specific week works best for you, we will make arrangements accordingly.

How do I get started with volunteering?

You can reach out to us using the contact form above or complete a volunteer application at the link provided below.

Can I volunteer in multiple roles?

Yes, many of our volunteers contribute in various capacities. We welcome and appreciate your willingness to serve in multiple roles.

How frequently will I receive scheduling notifications?

Volunteer schedules are distributed during the last week of each month.
