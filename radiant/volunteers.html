Volunteer Positions & Responsibilities

At Radiant Church, we believe in nurturing talent and empowering everyone through our structured volunteer program. Explore the different positions below to find the best fit for your skills and passion.

Audio Engineer (Broadcast and FOH)

Mix front-of-house audio and broadcast feeds to create an immersive worship experience. Responsibilities include being present for practices and services, leading sound check, troubleshooting issues, and ensuring a balanced audio mix during services.

Lighting Engineer (GrandMA3)

Design and execute lighting cues to enhance the worship atmosphere. Tasks include programming lighting scenes when needed, operating the GrandMA3 console, and coordinating with the team on transitions and other special service elements.

Video Director

Lead the video team by operating the video switcher and directing camera operators. Responsibilities include planning camera shots, managing live streaming, and ensuring professional-quality production.

Camera Operator

Operate high-quality cameras to capture stunning visuals. Responsibilities include setting up equipment, understanding the cameras and lenses, following directions from the video director, and ensuring high-quality footage.

Computer Graphics Operator

Manage all on-screen visuals such as graphics, videos, lower thirds, and LED wall content. Tasks include being present at practices and services, knowing ProPresenter7, coordinating with the creative team on graphics and videos, and operating graphic software during services.

Stage Manager

Oversee all stage activities and ensure the service runs smoothly. Responsibilities include managing the order of service, communicating with the worship team, handling props, and coordinating transitions throughout the service.

Photographer

Capture the essence of Radiant’s services and events through high-quality photography. Responsibilities include taking candid shots, editing images, and building a visual story that reflects our mission.

Online Host

Engage with the online audience during livestreams by moderating chats and interacting with viewers. Responsibilities include coordinating with technical teams, troubleshooting streaming-related issues, and ensuring an engaging virtual experience.
