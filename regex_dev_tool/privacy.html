Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how RegEX: Dev Tool protects your data.

Effective Date: July 9, 2025

Last Updated: July 9, 2025

Introduction

RegEX Dev Tool ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application RegEX Dev Tool (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

We want to be transparent about the data we collect and how we use it:

Information We DO NOT Collect:

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact information
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • We do not collect biometric data
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files

Information Stored Locally:

  • Regex patterns you create and save within the app
  • App preferences and settings
  • Test text you enter (stored temporarily during app use)

All data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally on your device is used solely to:

  • Provide the core functionality of the regex testing tool
  • Save your custom regex patterns for future use
  • Remember your app preferences and settings
  • Improve your user experience within the app

We do not use your information for advertising, marketing, or any commercial purposes beyond providing the app's functionality.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties for any purpose.

Export Functionality:

The app includes an export feature that allows you to export your saved regex patterns. This is entirely under your control - we only export data when you explicitly choose to do so, and the exported data goes directly to your chosen destination (email, cloud storage, etc.) without passing through our servers.

No Third-Party Analytics:

We do not use third-party analytics services that would collect or transmit your data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using iOS standard security practices
  • We do not transmit your data over the internet
  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • Regular app updates include security improvements

However, please remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Retention

Your data is retained locally on your device until you choose to delete it:

  • Saved regex patterns remain until you delete them individually or clear all data
  • App settings persist until you reset them or uninstall the app
  • Temporary data (like current test text) is cleared when you close the app

Deleting Your Data:

You can delete your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual patterns within the app
  • Using the "Delete All Patterns" feature in Settings
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all data)

Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove such information.

International Users

If you are using our App from outside the United States, please note that since all data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfer occurs. Your data remains in your jurisdiction under your control.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy within the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy
  • For significant changes, we may provide additional notice within the app

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
