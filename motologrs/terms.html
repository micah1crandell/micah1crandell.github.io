2. DESCRIPTION OF SERVICE

MotoLog RS is a motorcycle tracking and maintenance application that allows you to:

  • Track motorcycle rides with GPS, including live speed, distance, elevation, lean angle, and drag-strip timing (0-60 and quarter mile)
  • Log maintenance tasks and service schedules
  • Manage multiple motorcycles in your garage
  • Record fuel entries and expenses
  • Store ride history and statistics
  • Track installed components and parts with mileage or time-based replacement intervals
  • Scan fuel receipts or gas pump screens automatically with AI-assisted OCR (Premium)
  • Export your service logbook as PDF or CSV
  • Store vehicle documents (insurance, registration, title) locally in the Workshop Wallet
  • Generate shareable ride-stat graphics for social media
  • Use Home Screen and Lock Screen widgets for quick-glance fuel range, maintenance status, and live telemetry (Premium)
  • Invoke hands-free ride tracking and fuel logging via Siri shortcuts
  • Sync data across your Apple devices via iCloud CloudKit (Premium)
  • Customize the app's appearance with premium themes (Premium)

3. USER DATA AND PRIVACY

  • Most data is stored locally on your device
  • Optional sign-in uses Apple Sign In, authenticated via Google Firebase
  • Premium iCloud sync transmits data to Apple's private CloudKit database
  • The AI Smart Scanner (fuel receipts and gas pump screens) transmits a compressed image to Firebase Cloud Functions, which processes it with Google Gemini AI. If the cloud scan fails, the app automatically falls back to on-device OCR using Apple's Vision framework, which does not transmit any data externally.
  • Precise GPS coordinates are shared with Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com) to fetch local weather
  • Workshop Wallet documents — including insurance card images, registration images, vehicle title images, VIN, license plate, insurance carrier, and policy number — are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted to any server
  • The app schedules local push notifications as advance reminders before free-tier data reaches the 30-day visibility limit. These notifications are generated and stored on-device only; no data is transmitted to any server. Notification permission can be managed in iOS Settings.
  • Subscription status is stored in your device's Keychain for security
  • Free-tier ride, fuel, and service data older than 30 days is subject to a Feature Limitation: it is hidden from the in-app interface but is NOT deleted. It remains stored on your device and becomes fully accessible again upon upgrading to Premium. This is an intentional design choice, not data loss.
  • We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, pseudonymous usage data (session duration, screens visited, purchase events) to improve the App. No personally identifiable information — including your name, email, ride data, or location — is sent to the analytics service. Analytics collection is enabled by default and can be disabled at any time in Settings → Privacy → Share Anonymous Analytics. We do not use this data for advertising or share it with third parties.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties
  • See our Privacy Policy for full details
