What the iOS app supports

A practical meal planning workflow, not a fake kitchen miracle.

The site copy below matches the current iOS app and focuses on features that are actually supported in the release build.

AI meal planning chat

Ask for recipe ideas, substitutions, allergen-aware edits, and meal planning help. Chat can use selected app context and attached images when you choose to send them.

Seven-day meal plan

Arrange meals across an upcoming seven-day window and sync planned meals to Apple Calendar when you grant calendar permission.

Saved recipe library

Store recipes with ingredients, instructions, cook time, calories, difficulty, favorites, and optional images.

Grocery lists

Create and manage grocery lists in the app, then export unchecked items to Apple Reminders when you grant reminders permission.

Preferences and appliances

Save allergies, diets, dislikes, favorite cooking tools, and kitchen appliances so SPREADD can personalize suggestions.

Optional family sharing

Family plans unlock household sync for shared recipes, meal plans, grocery lists, member profiles, invites, and a shared AI usage pool.

Free AI starter limit

Free accounts receive 10 lifetime AI messages. Paid AI usage is measured over the last 7 days. Older AI requests stop counting as they pass 7 days old.

Safety and privacy

Designed for practical help, with clear limits.

Not medical or allergy advice

Always verify labels, ingredients, allergens, cooking temperatures, nutrition, and food safety before relying on a recipe or AI suggestion.

Analytics avoid user content

Analytics are intended for app health, onboarding, retention, subscription flow, and broad feature-use measurement, not recipe names, prompts, grocery items, or images.

Delete account data in app

The iOS app includes Profile > Settings > Delete Account & Cloud Data for the current user's cloud data.
