Troubleshooting

Connectivity Issues

App cannot find devices on my network

Ensure you have granted Local Network Privacy permissions. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy > Local Network and make sure "Control It" is toggled ON. Without this, the app cannot communicate with other devices on your WiFi.

HTTP Actions

"The Internet connection appears to be offline"

This generic iOS error often means the app is blocked from the local network (see above) or the target IP address is unreachable. Double-check your target IP and ensure your firewall isn't blocking port 80/443.

MIDI Issues

Triggers stop working when I lock my phone

Go to Settings > MIDI Triggers and ensure Listening is enabled. This starts a background audio session (silent track) to keep the app active. Note that this might pause other audio apps.

Collaborative Grids

"Permission Failure" when joining a grid

You must be signed into iCloud and have iCloud Drive enabled. The app uses your Apple ID for secure authentication.

Still stuck? Try force-quitting the app and restarting. If issues persist, check the "Console" logs in the app diagnostics (if available) or contact support.
