Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2025

1. What We Collect

  • iCloud Data (CloudKit).
    Grid layouts, action definitions, multistep macros, optional profile display name and avatar. Stored in your private iCloud database; we never see the plaintext.
  • Device Keychain.
    Apple-issued tokens used to authenticate with CloudKit. We never see your Apple ID password.
  • Support Emails.
    The email address and message you send when you contact us.

2. What We Don’t Collect

No in-app analytics, no advertising identifiers, no location tracking, no third-party ad SDKs.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To sync your content across devices you own (when iCloud sync is enabled).
  • To display your grids and actions inside the App.
  • To troubleshoot issues when you email support.

4. Legal Bases

If you reside in the EU/EEA or UK, we rely on these legal bases under the GDPR/UK GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the Service you request;
  • Legitimate interest — in maintaining and improving the Service;
  • Consent — when you explicitly opt-in to optional features.

5. CCPA Notices (California)

We do not “sell” personal information as defined by the CCPA. California residents may request disclosure or deletion of personal data by emailing micahcrandell.dev@gmail.com.

6. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us for deletion.

7. Security

Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. iCloud content is additionally protected by Apple’s server-side encryption. No security system is perfect, but we follow industry best practices.

8. Data Retention

Your CloudKit data is kept until you delete it in-app or close your iCloud account.

9. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your CloudKit data at any time in Settings → Account → “Delete Cloud Content”.
  • EU/EEA/UK residents: the right to data portability and to object to certain processing.
  • California residents: the right to know, delete, and non-discrimination.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Policy. Material changes will be posted here and in-app 14 days before they take effect.

11. Contact

Email privacy questions to micahcrandell.dev@gmail.com.
